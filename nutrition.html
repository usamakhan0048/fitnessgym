HEALTHY RECIPES

GRILLED CHICKEN SALAD

This grilled chicken salad is a perfect healthy meal, packed with lean protein and fresh vegetables, perfect for anyone looking to stay fit and eat clean.

Ingredients:
  • 2 chicken breasts (grilled)
  • 2 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Lemon juice (for dressing)

AVOCADO SMOOTHIE

A creamy, nutrient-dense smoothie that’s rich in healthy fats, fiber, and vitamins. This avocado smoothie will keep you energized throughout the day.

Ingredients:
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes

QUINOA AND VEGGIE STIR-FRY

This quinoa and veggie stir-fry is a wholesome, plant-based meal that’s quick to prepare and bursting with flavor, thanks to the variety of veggies and quinoa.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

HEALTHY EATING TIPS

  • Plan your meals with balanced nutrition, including proteins, carbs, and healthy fats to maintain energy and support body functions.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking at least 8 glasses of water a day to aid digestion and maintain overall health.
  • Incorporate more whole foods like fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins, while limiting processed foods high in sugars and unhealthy fats.
